Welcome to Beyond Earth

Space exploration is one of humanity's greatest adventures. From the first satellite in orbit to rovers crawling across Mars, we have been pushing the boundaries of what is possible since the dawn of the Space Age.

This site is a guide to the planets, the missions, the brave astronauts, and the incredible science behind humanity's journey beyond our home planet.
